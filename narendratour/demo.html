Q1How Much Does A Trip To Sri Lanka Cost?

Ans.For one week on average, the trip to Sri Lanka can cost 20,000 Indian rupees per person.

Q2.Is There Any Ravana Temple In Sri Lanka?

Ans.Koneswaram temple is a very famous Ravana Temple complex in Sri Lanka and it is the same place where Ravana and his mother are believed to have performed worship to Lord Shiva.

Q3.Is Sri Lanka More Expensive Than India?

Ans.No. India is 11.2% more expensive than Sri Lanka.

Q5.Is There A Sri Lanka Tour Package From Chennai?

Ans.Namaste India Trip offers several packages to explore Sri Lanka and the Sri Lanka tour package from Chennai is a bestselling option due to its proximity to the island nation.

Q5.What Does A Sri Lanka Ramayana Tour Map Look Like?

Ans.There are numerous places of Ramayana important to visit in Sri Lanka. A typical tour map of Sri Lanka for Ramayana will take you to places like Sigiriya Rock Fort, Ashok Vatika, Munneswaram Temple, Divurumpola Monastery, Chillaw, Colombo, Negombo, and many more.
