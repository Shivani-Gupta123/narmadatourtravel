]]> You can shape the start of your honeymoon with an enriching Indian experience that will last you and your partner for a lifetime. You’ll find a lot of custom fit India honeymoon packages so that you can do what you want to do at the pace you want to do it. This will enable you to relax with your partner as you enjoy the sights and sounds of India.

Kerala Backwater Honeymoon


Located south of India is one of the most serene states of India. If you are looking for the honeymoon in India that you always dreamed of, the scenic beauty and solemnity of Kerala is perfect. This hill station welcomes honeymooners with a cool climate and lush vegetation. Couples can walk hand in hand as they explore neatly lined tea plantations. The hills station has numerous of sanctuaries where the couple can discover rare species of birds and other fauna. You’ll find a lot of national parks and wildlife sanctuaries.

The backwaters of Kerala make a honeymoon interesting. Couples can rent a houseboat for a long ride overnight. Houseboats are furnished with a kitchen, bedroom and lounge area. You’ll have a ferryman to guide the boat as it travels under the Indian sky and a cook who’ll serve you exotic Indian cuisine. You can enjoy the paddies and backwaters of Kollam and Alleppey, rest at the Kovalam beach and Munnar Hills, feel rejuvenated at the Athirapally Waterfalls and visit the Periyar Sanctuary.

Goa Beach Honeymoon

Beach lovers can celebrate their honeymoon on a secluded area of Goa’s beaches. This prime spot has a lot of recreational sites and raving parties at night that last till dawn. The coastline of Goa stretches to around 100 kilometers.

Goa has a little of everything for the variety of honeymooners that visit it shores. You’ll find old relics, monuments, flea markets, cozy cottages and villas that you can rent and a lot of water activities that you can enjoy together with your partner. Water activities include water skiing, parasailing and diving. You can also tuck into Goa’s excellent cuisine while enjoying idyllic sceneries.

The famous beaches of Goa include Baga, Cavelossim, Anjuna, Chapora, Calangute and Dona Paula. They have great pubs and exceptionally great sunsets which are perfect for newlyweds. Couples can enjoy the great ambiance and the culture which includes infamous all fighter parties to celebrate your honeymoon in India.

Natural Shimla Honeymoon

A favorite destination for both local and foreign honeymooners, Shimla takes you to a whole new experience of majestic mountains and falling snow.

The Himachal Pradesh, the land of eternal snow peaks provides a picturesque setting for honeymooning couples. The variety of landscapes has a lot to offer as it is a habitat to a lot of flora and fauna. The natural beauty of this region lets couples enjoy the Mother Nature at her best. Couples can bond over a couple of adventure sports as the valley and plains of Shimla provide an excellent playground. The transition of plains to astonishingly high peaks such as Lahaul and Spiti encases numerous valleys and pits where you can enjoy a lot of great views during your honeymoon Indian.

Customizing your own Indian honeymoon lets you travel and explore India at your own pace. You’ll find that you can enjoy nature at its best without having to forego the modern comforts that you’ll need on your honeymoon. In fact, you can enjoy a luxurious and elegant experience in the ways of the Indians.

]]> South India is a heaven in itself, where you have everything set for an incredible holiday. You have the hills, backwaters, sea, city, temples, and villages. Thus, South India is no doubt a honeymooner’s paradise. Newlyweds who are in search of some serenity and privacy can choose to visit South India.

South India consists of Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu. And each of these states has a number of stunning beaches and hill stations, apart from high-quality infrastructure and top-notch accommodation facility.

Mentioned below are some of the most popular honeymoon destinations in South India:

1. Munnar, Kerala:

Located in Kerala, Munnar is a well-known hill station that offers a unique romantic appeal. With the stunning hill ranges, the fuzzy breeze, the pleasant weather, and the serene lakes, Munnar is the place to get lost in the lap of nature and revive in the most romantic manner. When in Munnar, couples can visit Blossom International Park, Tata Tea Museum collection, Rajamalai-Eravikulam National Park, as well as Mattupetty dam.

2. Ooty, Tamil Nadu:

Located in Tamil Nadu, Ooty is a scenic hill station and is fondly known as the “Queen of Hill Stations.” Ooty is well-known for its lush green tropical forests, romantic boat-rides, the toy train ride, and the rich aroma of tea and coffee plantations. This place has a number of resorts and hotels that provide best in class accommodation and fooding facilities to the people.

3. Belekeri, Karnataka:

Situated in Karnataka, Belekeri beach offers honeymooners a serene environment that is filled with love and romance. The pristine expanse of white sandy beach, refreshing breeze, and attractive coast-line dotted with coconut plantations are some of the things to look out for when at Belekeri.

4. Coorg, Karnataka:

Located in Karnataka, Coorg is well-known for the mountains and the aromatic scent of coffee. Surrounded by the mountain ranges of Brahmagiri, Pushpagiri, and Tadiandamol, Coorg is an outstanding tourist spot that has a rural romantic charm. Iruppu Falls, Abbey Falls, Harangi Dam, and Honnamana Kere Lake are some of the places to visit in Coorg. Couples who are adventure lovers can take part in microlight flying or white water rafting too.

5. Pondicherry, Tamil Nadu:

Pondicherry is one of most beautiful honeymoon destinations in India for the newlyweds. Some of the places to visit in Pondicherry include Auroville Ashram, Paradise Beach, and Seaside Promenade.

6. Araku Valley, Andhra Pradesh:

This is one of the most popular destinations located in Andhra Pradesh for the honeymooners. Some of the most popular places to visit here include Ananthagiri, Borra Caves, Padmapuram Gardens, Tribal Museum, Duduma Waterfalls, etc.

7. Kumarakom, Kerala:

Located in Kerala, Kumarakom is a tranquil honeymoon destination that is decorated with enthralling waterscapes. A houseboat ride on the endless stretch of backwaters is one of the most amazing activities that newlyweds can take part in.

8. Wayanad, Kerala:

With its thick rain forest, stunning waterfalls, and spice plantations, Wayanad in Kerala is the ideal destination for an intimate yet exciting honeymoon. The popular places to visit in Wayanad include Kuruva Island, Chembra Peak, Meenmutty Waterfalls, Edakkal Caves, etc.

9. Meghamalai, Tamil Nadu:

Situated in Tamil Nadu, Meghamalai boasts of tranquil landscapes and pleasant sceneries. Being an off-beat destination, this place offers complete privacy to the newlyweds and is ideal for couples who love peace and tranquility.

These are some of the most popular places for the honeymooners to visit in South India.

In every couple’s, honeymoon is a special occasion that deserves special treatments. Therefore, treat yourself to one of these incredible honeymoon destinations that would not only leave a long-lasting impression on your mind, but will also help in strengthening the bond of love with your partner.
