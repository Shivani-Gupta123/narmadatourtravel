30 Best 5 Star Hotels near me in Bangalore

Bangalore or Bengaluru is the capital of Karnataka and is also known as the Silicon Valley of the subcontinent. The south Indian state is known for being the home ground for some of the most iconic brand names in India and the world. However, Bangalore is more than just an IT hub. This city of the future is one of the most loved holiday destinations among travelers. From the tech parks to state-of-the-art buildings and monuments, this metro city is filled with homely folks and memorable moments just around the corner. The state is famous for its art, culture, art galleries, and amazing rock music and foodie culture. The wildlife sanctuaries and the nearby hill stations also deserve special mention.

The next time you are on a holiday in Bangalore, here are the 30 best 5-star hotels in Bangalore to make your holiday here even more luxurious and comfortable.

List of the 30 best 5-Star Hotels & Resorts near me in Bangalore

1. The Ritz Carlton

Ritz Carlton ranks among one of the highest-rated luxury hotels in Bangalore. Say goodbye to all the hustle and bustle of city life by booking a stay at the Ritz Carlton. This 5-star hotel in Bangalore is located in very close proximity to various tourist attractions of the city like MG Road, Cubbon Park, UB city, and other such places. The hotel represents contemporary elegance through its display of intricate 1200 artworks. It is home to 7 fine-dining restaurants and bars, a stunning rooftop bar, a luxury spa, and a wellness floor.

2. The Oberoi

The Oberoi group of 5-star hotels is one of the most prestigious names in the luxury hotel industry. In Bangalore, it is situated in the heart of the city on MG Road. It boasts of displaying one of the most classy structures complete with impeccable views of the ages-old rain trees garden. Other than top-notch services and valuable services, The Oberoi is also known for its impeccably designed and aesthetically sound rooms. They even come with balconies and a small kitchenette. The Bangalorean business district and shopping malls are all located at a reachable distance making your stay experience convenient. Other extra services that add a lot of value to the hotel are valet parking, pick-up, and drop-off service, and a range of fine dining restaurants.

3. Taj West End

When it comes to luxury 5-star hotels in India, Taj is one name that cannot go unmentioned. The Taj West End in Bangalore is located in the center of the city. It showcases the stunning old-world beauty through its colonial-style architectural brilliance. The magnificent property covers a massive area of over 20 acres complete with lush gardens and a charm that cannot be found in any other 5-star luxury properties across India. All the rooms in Taj West End are designed and decorated in a traditional manner that gives it a homely and cozy vibe. The standout feature is still the fine dining restaurant and an incredibly well-stocked bar.

4. ITC Gardenia

As the name suggests, ITC Gardenia in Bangalore is an ode to the lush gardens of Bangalore. Located centrally in Bangalore, this 5-star property is all about representing the greenery Bangalore is home to. It houses one of the largest poolside and garden lounging areas in all of Bengaluru resorts. Every inch of the hotel is tastefully decorated and very well equipped with stunning terraces, living areas, and rooms with finely upholstered furnishings. The best room here is the honeymoon suite. From the restaurant to the bar, every aspect of this hotel is an experience in itself. Unwind here by getting an all-natural and personal treatment at the organic spa inside the hotel and having the experience of a lifetime.

5. Palm Meadows Club

The Palm Meadows Club in Bangalore offers a tranquil getaway from the bustle of city life. The luxury of this 5-star property blends in perfectly with the relaxing vibe the hotel has to offer. The palm is covered over an area of 5 acres but its luxuries go above and beyond. From corporate and business-related getaways to weddings and relaxing staycations this hotel has options for all. Along with state-of-the-art facilities to be located very close to the IT corridor Whitefield and just one hour away from the international airport, this hotel is much loved by all different types of travelers. This 5-star property in Bangalore offers some of the most indulgent experiences along with a plethora of fine dining options. Overall a stay at the Palm Meadows Club will be a memorable one.

6. The Leela Palace

With the history of the Leela Palace dating back to 1986 in Mumbai, this luxurious 5-star hotel has spread its wings across the subcontinent and beyond. Headquartered in Mumbai, the Leela Palace Bangalore is all about showcasing unparalleled experience through its environs, services, and overall vibe. Occupying over an area of 7 massive acres, the hotel houses a variety of rooms that include lavish suites, comfortable and inviting deluxe rooms, and more. The award-winning restaurant can be your go-to option for food during your stay here. It would be a great idea to relax and unwind at the numerous spa services the hotel has to offer. Complete with an outdoor pool, a well-stocked library, and a bar, the Leela Palace in Bangalore will be a wonderful stay option for all.

7. Sheraton Grand Bengaluru

Surrounded by the top local tourist destinations in Bengaluru, Sheraton Grand is family-friendly and one of the top-ranking 5-star hotels in Bangalore. Just a short drive away from the international airport the luxury hotel represents the vibrancy and charm of Karnataka in one sphere. With luxurious bedding, grand bathrooms, and scenic views of the cityscapes, this hotel is truly a mesmerizing experience. The hotel restaurants and bar are extremely well-equipped with top-notch brands of liquor and a well-thought-out menu. The hotel also hosts interesting and fun events in the pillarless ballroom which is truly a one-of-a-kind experience. The fitness center will ensure you dont miss a single day at the gym and the massage center will ensure a restorative experience.

8. Conrad Bengaluru

Conrad Bengaluru is all about offering a combination of both elegance and opulence with comfort and warmth. This 24-story tall building is a state-of-the-art structure and oversees the business hub. What captures the attention of every guest at the hotel is the variety of food offered at the 5 different restaurants serving European cuisines and even a gluten-free option for breakfast. The infinity pool and the fitness center act as luxurious add-ons. Other than that the bar and the sauna make the whole stay experience here truly worth it. They have options available to cater to the business crowd as well as people on vacations or honeymoon.

9. Shangri-La

The term Shangri La means an imaginary paradise on earth or a remote and exotic utopia. That is exactly what Shangri La’s 5-star property in Bangalore represents. This modern and stunning getaway destination in Bangalore is located well above sea level which enables awesome panoramic views of the charming Bangalore palace. The hotel is extremely popular for its rooftop restaurant, lounge, and bar. The hotel also hosts 7 live cooking stations which elevates the dining experience even more. Every suite is well and thoughtfully decorated to maximize the luxury experience. No matter where you are coming from, Shangri La Bangalore will guarantee you an experience to remember.

10. The Aloft Bengaluru

Situated in the busy outer ring road part of the Silicon Valley of India, Bangalore, an aloft hotel offers a chic atmosphere and a tech-savvy space for all travelers. The vibrant social scene here combined with the buzzing outdoor IT corridor makes it an ideal choice for techies visiting Bangalore on holiday or business-related trips. The hotels all-day restaurant, Nook, is one of the best in the region. After exploring the tourist hub of Bellandur, you can recharge your mind, body, and soul at the 24-hour fitness center or by going for a swim in the outdoor pool. The stand-out feature for the rooms at Aloft is that all the rooms here come equipped with complimentary Wi-Fi service, classic bedding, a walk-in shower, and other such luxurious touches that make a stay here totally worthwhile.

11. Renaissance Bengaluru Race Course Hotel

Nestled within the central business district of Bangalore and just 500 meters away from the Bangalore race course this hotel unravels a lesser-explored side of the Silicon Valley of India. Renaissance Bengaluru is an ideal hotel for every professional including IT folks and even medical professionals. The hotel serves all the purposes of a traveller as it is situated in a very strategic location that offers the opportunity to shop at the palace grounds or explore the Koramangala nightlife. The unique and charming hotel amenities such as a rooftop pool and a 24-hour fitness center are guaranteed to add some x-factor to your trips to Bangalore.

12. The Taj Residency

Taj Residency is situated in the famous MG road of the city which has got to be the most happening part. The luxury 5-star hotel wonderfully reflects the citys cosmopolitan nature through its modern contemporary decor. From the new age furnishings to its chic interiors, every Inc of this hotel is designed to match the aesthetics of its esteemed travelers. The location of this hotel plays a major role in making it a favorite among travelers since it is equidistant from Commercial Street, the tourist attraction of Lal Bagh, malls, and more. You will fall in love with the city if you decide to stay at this amazing hotel.

13. Radisson Blu Atria

Radisson Blu Atria occupies the heart of Bangalore and offers one of the most upscale stay options for everyone. The easy access to the central business district makes it a favorite among business and IT guests. Just 40 minutes away from the international airport and also near shopping places, this hotel is a great choice for holiday accommodation. Remember to spare some time to enjoy the in-house spa treatment and have a fun time in the outdoor pool. Whether you are hosting a business meeting or looking for a wedding venue then Radisson Atria is a trustworthy name. Make your stay in Bangalore worthwhile with one of the most trusted names in the 5-star hotel industry.

14. Goldfinch Hotel

Goldfinch Bangalore is the flagship hotel belonging to the MRG group. The one thing that will strike you first is the exquisite and chic interiors. This blend of comfort and contemporary sophistication works perfectly in providing a memorable stay. Oftentimes goldfinch hotels are the first choice for leisure as well as business travellers. The rooms are extremely classy and come with modern amenities. The banquet halls are extremely spacious and the soothing wellness center provides relief from stressful travel days. The restaurants of this hotel have a fan base of its own. Every dish is designed and curated keeping in mind the customers needs. A stay at Goldfinch is guaranteed to be a wholesome one.

15. The Lalit Ashok

Spread out in a massive area of 10 acres, the Lalit Ashok is one of the most classy and sophisticated 5-star hotels in the region. The sprawling landscape feels like a treat for all the senses from the moment you step in. The hotel premise contains well-manicured lawns and rooms that wonderfully overlook the in-house pool and a lavish golf course. Every room is very stylishly decorated and completed by adding modern contemporary amenities like a flat-screen TV, a cozy little sitting area, a well-maintained bathroom, and others. There is even a fully functional tennis court and various rejuvenating Ayurvedic Spa treatments. The Lalit Ashok is like a world in itself.

16. Four Seasons Hotel Bengaluru at Embassy One

The Four Seasons Hotel in Bangalore offers an unparalleled living experience during your holiday in the city. The hotel is home to 109 different rooms that span across 2 different towers. Each aspect of this 5-star luxury hotel in Bangalore provides stylish, spacious, and well-equipped living spaces. The 30-story tower also comes with dedicated high-speed elevators, wooden paneling, a private lobby, and other such interesting features that make this 5-star accommodation truly a memorable stay option. The elegance and the world-class level of service with top-notch restaurants make Four Seasons a trustworthy hotel name.

17. Hilton Bengaluru Embassy

Situated within the heart of Bangalore city near embassy golf links Tech Park and other such IT tech parks, the Hilton Bengaluru embassy is all about giving the guests a memorable stay experience. From enjoying drinks at the well-stocked bar and sipping delicious coffee at the in-house cafe to dining at one of the best restaurants, a stay at this hotel will remain close to your heart for a long time. The rooms are designed with sleek interiors, stylish settings, and a modern contemporary vibe that upholds the sophisticated vibe of the hotel as a whole.

18. Le Meridien

Situated just 500 meters away from the Bangalore Modern Art Gallery, hotel Le Meridien Bangalore is truly a one-of-a-kind luxury stay experience. It is one of the rare hotels that lies conveniently at the crossroads of airports, railways, and other such connecting transportation services. The hotel offers a range of rooms that will suit your budgetary needs. The hotels multi-cuisine restaurant remains open 24 hours so whenever you are getting the munchies you can savour the best food. The cherry on the cake for the restaurant is its live musical performance.

19. The Zuri Whitefield

Just 45 minutes away from the international airport, the Zuri Whitefield is located amidst the business locality of the city yet offers a very peaceful environment, away from the bustle of city life. This chic business hotel offers the guests a range of top-notch hospitality, services, and sophisticated culture. Due to its location, the hotel has managed to showcase the rich essence of this vibrant city. The hotel is also optimized in a way that panders to the needs of both leisure as well as business travelers. Every room at the hotel is designed to give the guests the most comfortable stay experience.

20. Hotel Royal Orchid

The 5-star property of the Hotel Royal Orchid is situated in the Indiranagar region of the city of Bangalore. The hotel houses 195 different rooms and suites that offer stunning and soul-stirring views of the nearby golf course. It is one of the only hotels in the city that offers a temperature-controlled swimming pool. The luxury banquet and the massive well-maintained lawns here can cater to 2,000 guests making it an ideal destination to host weddings. The hotels main feature is its amazing selection of cafes and award-winning restaurants that will leave the guests asking for more.

The hotel is very carefully designed and offers unlimited levels of luxury and relaxation to all types of travelers from different corners of the world. 22. The Crowne Plaza

Nestled peacefully within the bustling electronic city part of Bangalore, the Crowne Plaza is the perfect choice for a 5-star level of holiday. What attracts the guests to this hotel is its incredible range of top-notch amenities coupled with some of the best high-end staff services. The rooms and suites are designed in such a way that they offer the utmost comfort and coziness to every guest. The outdoor swimming pool and the fitness center are built to provide all the guests with a premium stay experience where they do not miss out on their fitness regime.

The Chancery Pavilion is a premium hotel that occupies a central space in Bangalores famous business district. It is the ideal sojourn for all modern-day travelers from across the globe. The hospitality of the staff combined with the modern ambiance makes a stay here totally worthwhile. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for Best 5 Star Hotels in Bangalore

Q1. When Is The Best Time To Visit Bangalore?

Ans. October to February is the best time to visit Bangalore.

Q2. What Is Bangalore Famous For?

Ans. Bangalore is famous as the Silicon Valley of India and is a major software exporter of semiconductors.

Q3. Which Places Can I Visit If I Have 1 Day In Bangalore?

Ans. You can cover places like Cubbon Park, Bangalore Fort, Lal Bagh Garden, Kote Venkateshwara Temple, and others.

Q4. Where to Shop In Bangalore?

Ans. Some of the best shopping places in Bangalore are Commercial Street, Chickpet, Jayanagar 4th block, Dubai Plaza, Brigade Road, etc.

Q5. Which Are The Best Hill Stations Near Bangalore?

Ans. Nandi Hills, Chikmagalur, Yelagiri, Wayanad, Savandurga, Coorg, etc. are some of the most famous hill stations near Bangalore.
