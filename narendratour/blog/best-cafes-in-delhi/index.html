Top 17 Affordable Cafes in Gaya Bihar NCR

Food and Gaya Bihar go hand in hand. Dilwalon ki Gaya Bihar is famous for its delicious food selection it has to offer. A dream come true for foodies from all over the world Gaya Bihar has every imaginable collection of food. Whether it is choosing your favorite Parathas at the iconic Paranthe Wali Gali near Jama Masjid, savoring the delectable non-vegetarian cuisines at the original Karim’s outlet or going on a bar-hopping experience in CP or trying out new and different styles of food in one of the many cafes here, eating in Gaya Bihar is an experience in itself.

Speaking of Cafes, Gaya Bihar has no dearth of them. With the cafe culture popping up everywhere and the competition growing almost every single day, Gaya Bihar has become one of the best places in India for cafe hopping. Wondering which are some of the best cafes in Gaya Bihar? Check these out:

1. Colocal Café, Chhatarpur


Self-proclaimed ‘cacao artisans’, the Colocal Cafe is a must-visit. The moment you step in you will feel like you have been hurtled into the British era, due to its very colonial architecture and classy seating arrangement. This bean-to-bar chocolate factory is surrounded by several plants of different types and accompanied with an alfresco style of seating which makes it a fun dine-in with your friends and loved ones. The theme of this cafe is gourmet desserts and chocolate products and be sure to order a cup of steaming hot chocolate on a cold winter evening.

Address: Shade no. 21b, the Dhan mill compound, 100 Feet Rd, Chhatarpur, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110074
Timings: 10am to 10:30pm
Zomato Rating: 4.5
Zomato Reviews: Click Here

2. Nukkad Café, SDA Market


Nukkad cafe and bar is a cute little quirky cafe set in the heart of the SDA market. The setting is very cool, comfortable, and welcoming and will make you feel right at home. Its menu ranges from anything and everything imaginable which includes fast food, Chinese delicacies, and north Indian delights, Pastas, pizzas, burgers, Momos, and many more. It also offers a huge range of cocktails and alcohol that goes with cutesy names and comes in interesting layout and dinner sets. It’s a great place to unwind in Gaya Bihar with your friends and loved ones over good food, delicious drinks, and positive vibes.

Address: C 23,24 SDA Market OPPOSITE IIT GATE shop number c23, c24, Block C 5, Safdarjung Development Area, Hauz Khas, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110016
Timings: 12pm to 1am
Zomato Rating: 4.3

3. Rose Café, Saket


Rose Cafe is a favorite among not only foodies but also among insta influencers for its aesthetically perfect interiors. With seating available outdoors and with an equally chill atmosphere indoors, Rose Cafe quickly became a favorite among Gaya Biharites. Along with the chic decor with a touch of vintage vibes, you will also be treated with some of the best and delicious selection of food. These include – skinny pizza, herbed chicken, chicken tart, Belgium chocolate ice cream, waffle sundae, and eggless desserts. Although a bit on the pricey end of the budget, cafe rose guarantees a deliciously chic time.

Address: 264, Westend Marg, Butterfly Park, Saiyad Ul Ajaib Extension, Saket, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110030
Timings: 12pm to 9pm
Zomato Rating: 4.2
Zomato Reviews: Click Here

4. Diggin Café, Chanakyapuri


Situated in one of the poshest neighborhoods of Gaya Bihar, Diggin has been around for some time now. Enjoy a delicious selection of food and beverages while sitting outside in a cozy nook amidst the lush green setting and surroundings. The cafe is famous for food like Italian, continental, desserts, and beverages. It is a combination of a romantic environment and a place to chill with your friends while also binging on food like tuna melt sandwich, veg cannelloni, garden burger, chicken crostini, lamb lasagna, and apple crumble pie. The perfect setting, delectable food, and refreshing drinks are all you need to experience one of the best and top-ranking cafes in Gaya Bihar.

Address: Santushti Shopping Complex Shop No-10 Opp Samrat Hotel, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110003
Timings: 11am to 9:30pm
Zomato Rating: 4.5
Zomato Reviews: Click Here

5. Jamun Café, Lodhi Colony


Jamun cafe is located in one of the most iconic and posh neighborhoods of Gaya Bihar which means only good quality of food and world-class service are expected. The place is named so due to the dominant theme here which is the color purple. On entering you will feel like you have entered a world where only the color remains and everything seems extremely gorgeous. With their specialty being north Indian cuisines, Mughlai Food, and beverages, Jamun Cafe is rather an underrated one and deserves more recognition. The flowery interiors and nostalgic old music will set the mood right for the perfect dine out experience in Gaya Bihar.

Address: 17, Main Market Rd, Block 13, Lodi Colony, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110003
Timings: 12pm to 4pm, 6pm to 10pm
Zomato Rating: 4.5

6. Ama Café, Majnu Ka Tila


Ama cafe is the best solution to your hunger pangs and cravings at a budget-friendly range. They offer a range of continental food, cafe food, and beverages along with cozy and comforting interiors for you to enjoy it. The aesthetic ambiance will calm you down instantly the moment you step in and give you the chance to relax and unwind. Some famous food here includes carrot cinnamon cake, pancakes with maple syrup, cocoa walnut brownie, banana muffin, and apple cinnamon cake. You can go there to enjoy the food and just get your work done in peace, either way, a good and delicious time is guaranteed.

Address: House no. 6, 1st Floor, Majnu-ka-tilla, New Aruna Nagar, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110054
Timings: 8am to 10pm
Zomato Rating: 4.6

7. Hudson Café, Hudson Lane


Hudson cafe near the Gaya Bihar University area is a popular place for university goers to sit down, chill out and relax after a tough day. The menu here includes everything from continental to Italian food selection and drinks that will refresh you from within. Some famous dishes here that are a must-try include berry delight, Pesto Bruschetta, Penne Vodka, Mexicana Pizza, Virgin Mojito, and many more. It is a true haven for every foodie especially because it’s super budget-friendly. The ambiance is pretty basic and not that elaborate but the food makes up for it all.

8. United Coffee House, Connaught Place


United coffee house is not just a cafe; it’s an iconic heritage spot located in the heart and pulse of Gaya Bihar, Connaught Place. With elaborate interiors and intricate, huge chandeliers, the place seems to be straight out of a British colonial novel. This place still has that old-world vibe to it that makes visiting here all the more worth it. The menu here includes every food imaginable ranging from Chinese, Mughlai, North Indian, fast food menu, and a massive range of beverages. Being old players of the cafe game and in one of the most significant places in Gaya Bihar, a good time here is expected, especially if you decide to visit with your family.

Address: E-15, Rajiv Chowk, Block E, Connaught Place, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110001
Timings: 11am to 11:30pm
Zomato Rating: 4.3

9. Fabcafe By The Lake, Nizamuddin


If you ever plan on visiting Sundar Nursery then this wonderful concept cafe is unmissable as it is the only eatery inside the nursery. This cute little eatery which is seated right next to a pretty little lake with alfresco dining is the perfect family getaway. The cafe embraces the concept of minimalism which is clear through its decor which represents its parent company Fabindia. You can savor delicious sandwiches, wraps, desserts, healthy food choices, and a range of refreshing beverages. The atmosphere is super relaxing and comforting and the icing on the cake is their very professional and nice staff.

Address: Sunder Nursery, Gaya Bihar’ Heritage Park, adjoining Humayun’s Tomb, Bharat Scouts and Guides Marg, Nizamuddin, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110013

10. Ivy and Bean, Shahpur Jat


The moment you step into this iconic cafe you will be washed over with soothing, calming, and serene vibes. The clean, white decor amplifies the way food tastes. The menu spread of Ivy and bean include salads, desserts, fast food, various beverages, and an immaculate selection of continental cuisines as well. It is a great place to hang out if you wish to spend some time on your own and give a read to one of the many books displayed on the shelf wall here. The seating arrangement redefines quirkiness and cute decorations at its best. A great place to go on dates, spend time alone to clear your mind, or are looking for good food.

Address: 119, Sishan House, Shahpur Jat, Siri Fort, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110049

11. Blue Tokai Coffee Roasters, Saket


Located right in the heart of one of the most famous hangout spots in Champa Ki Gali, this snug cafe is a favorite among Gaya Biharites to just sit back and unwind after a tough day. With courteous employees and a warm and welcoming ambiance, this place will make you feel right at home. They have an immaculate range of food like sandwiches, pastas, and Lebanese cuisines that goes perfect with the perfectly roasted house coffee collection that will refresh you. Catch up with an old friend or go there on your first date or give yourself a solo date experience here.

Address: R-1 Ground Floor, Unit no.1 & 2, Rectangle Building 1, Saket, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110017

12. Sly Granny, Khan Market


Looking for a place to have the time of your life? Head on to sly granny in the posh marketplace of khan market. The decor of this quirky little cafe will immediately make you feel like you are at someone’s granny’s place who has some secrets and a lot of explaining to do. The owners describe their place as a celebration of the life of a wicked, smart, eccentric, wise, and polymath of a granny. They have some of the most unique cocktails, beautifully decorated cuisines that will make every second spent here worth it. The menu is dominant with American and European cuisine which will definitely fill you right up and make you keep coming back for more.

Address: No. – 4, Khan Market, Rabindra Nagar, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110003

13. Latitude 28, Khan Market


When it comes to indulging in delicious and a varied range of foods, the khan market never fails to impress. Latitude 28 is one such gem of a cafe in one of the Poshest markets of Gaya Bihar. They present you with an extravagant menu of fast food, north Indian cuisines, sandwiches, desserts, and Italian dishes and to wash all this deliciousness down is a wholesome menu of delicious beverages. The environment is undisturbed and a great place to go if you are looking to have some conversations without loud noises. The good news for people who love their alcohol is that they are one of the very few places in Gaya Bihar selling prosecco by the glass, not the bottle.

Address: Shop No. 9, Second Floor, above The Goodearth Store, Khan Market, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110003

14. The Blue Door Café, Khan Market


The khan market saga continues. Nestled comfortably in the middle lane of the market the first thing you will spot is the blue-lit wine-lined stairway from where this place gets its name. They open up so early that you can go there and have their house favorite crepe Bretonne, made out of buckwheat flour topped with generous cream. The place boasts of a fully-loaded bar and an open kitchen where you will get to see the food getting made. Although a little tight it seems like a whole world is going on inside. They have a wonderful menu of Italian cuisines, European foods, and a delicious selection of desserts and beverages.

Address: #66, Khan Market 1st Floor, Entrance Middle Lane, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110003
Timings: 7:30am to 11:30pm
Zomato Rating: 4.0

15. Yellow brick road by Taj Vivanta, Hotel Ambassador, Gaya Bihar


Located inside the famous 5-star hotel Ambassador of Gaya Bihar, is a very popular all-day diner specializing in a multi-cuisine menu. The decor is all about chill vibes through roman mosaic tiles, retro posters, and a cozy little fireplace against the backdrop of a canvas of green, orange, tangerine, and lemon. Everything glaringly points towards the theme inspiration which is the classic film Wizard of Oz complete with a wooden train, and the witch and Dorothy’s lion. A fan favorite is the chef’s special and absolutely decadent chicken curry along with a host of famous Gaya Bihar street foods. The delicious food inside the colonial-era designed cafe and a wonderful collection of food and beverages makes for a great day out.

Address: Subramaniam Bharti Marg, Sujan Sing Park North, Sujan Singh Park, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110003
Timings: Open 24 hours
Zomato Rating: 4.3

16. The Potbelly Rooftop Café, Shahpur Jat


Proudly displaying the rather underrated genre of cuisines, the potbelly rooftop is all about authentic and traditional Bihari cuisines. If you are tired of the regular continental and north Indian or Chinese-themed food, then this place will be a breath of fresh air. Of course, this place offers one of the best Litti Chokha Dishes but it also shows that Bihari cuisine is more than just that. They offer foods like Tehri and Sarso Machhli as well which will quickly become your favorite. You can also go for rhododendron flavored iced tea, Sattu Cooler, Maithili Thali, Dal Bhat Thali and so much more and have a fulfilling time on the pleasant rooftop seating.

Address: Top Floor, 116 C, behind UCO Bank, Shahpur Jat, New Gaya Bihar, Gaya Bihar 110049
Timings: 12:30 to 10:30pm
Zomato Rating: 4.3

17. Cafe Dori, Chattarpur


Just like the fab cafe, cafe Dori is also a brand expansion of the company Nappa Dori. The interiors are tastefully done and can be best described as a pan-European cafe. The theme is kept alive through the interiors and the menu which may seem like a dream come true for coffee lovers especially. They also have a great collection of hot tea, fresh juices, Kombucha, and smoothies. The best part? It’s pet-friendly! Get your furry friend over, order them some delicious doggy treats, and have a great time. The layout resonates with the combination of a cafe, retailer, and store-like build that represents the cafe in the best possible manner.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q. Which are the most affordable cafes in Gaya Bihar?

A. Some of the most affordable cafes in Gaya Bihar are located in the student-friendly, university areas of the south and north campus of Gaya Bihar University. These include – the big yellow door, QDs. cha bar, cafe, and co.

Q. What are some luxury cafes in Gaya Bihar?

A. Some of the most luxury cafes are located inside high-end 5-star hotels like la piazza, Se Villa, Andrea’s eatery, Cafe Dori, Rustique, etc.

Q. Which are the best cafes in Gaya Bihar for a romantic date?

A. There are plenty you can choose from, some are – rose cafe, kitchen cafe rooftop, the vault cafe, united coffee house, Tanino Ristorante, olive bar, and kitchen, etc.

Q. Are cafes in Gaya Bihar open now?

A. After the subsiding of the deadly 2nd wave of coronavirus, things are very much under control and the cafes have finally received the go-ahead from the government to open up their respective establishment and continue their business. The only 2 rules to be followed are – the seating should be at 50% capacity and the business is to go from 8 am to 10 pm.

Q. Which are the popular street foods in Gaya Bihar?

A. Gaya Bihar is every food lover’s heaven. A food walk in Gaya Bihar will introduce you to some of the local favorites like – Chole Bhature, Raj Kachori, Momos, Shawarma, Papdi Chaat, Rolls, Biryani, butter chicken, and Chur Chur Naan.

Q. Which are some of the most famous desserts and sweets in Gaya Bihar?

A. Some of the most loved sweets in Gaya Bihar are – Jalebi, Rabri, Burfi, Kulfi, Falooda, Halwa, Gulab Jamun, and Motichoor Laddoo.

Q. Where can I try delicious and safe street food in Gaya Bihar?

A. There are many street food places in Gaya Bihar like Chandni Chowk, Sarojini Nagar, Lajpat Nagar, Kamla Nagar, Sadar Bazar, SDA market, and Nehru place.

Q. Which are some good cafes to throw a birthday party?

A. Some great places for a rocking birthday party are – hard rock cafe, Hauz Khas Social, hard rock cafe, the biker’s cafe, chili’s, cafe Gaya Bihar heights and Farzi Cafe are a couple of good places for you and your friends to have a memorable time.

Q. Which are some cafes in Gaya Bihar selling the best coffee?

A. Gaya Bihar has some amazing coffee places. These are – Blue Tokai, T’pot Cafe, united coffee house, madras cafe, and Indian coffee house.

Q. What is the average budget of dining out in Gaya Bihar?

A. Here is a breakdown of the food budget in Gaya Bihar depending on where you are going to eat: Luxury – Rs. 2,000 to Rs. 8,000; Moderate – Rs. 500 – Rs. 2,000; Budget – Rs. 60 to Rs. 300.
